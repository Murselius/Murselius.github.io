A Fighting Card Game


Momentum is an electrifying two-player living card game that thrusts you into fierce martial combat! Master a variety of martial techniques to outwit your opponent. Harness the momentum, maintain relentless tempo, and unleash jaw-dropping combos to dominate the battlefield. But beware— lose your tempo, and the tides of battle could swiftly turn against you!

the Developer


Anssi Janttonen is a dedicated student at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences in Finland. His journey into the world of board games began approximately five years ago when his fiancée introduced him to the thriving board game community in Joensuu. What started as a casual interest quickly evolved into a deep passion, leading Anssi to explore game mechanics, strategy, and design at a more intricate level.

Over the years, he has immersed himself in the hobby, analyzing various games and their mechanics while refining his own ideas. His dedication and curiosity have driven him to experiment with unique gameplay concepts, culminating in the development of Momentum, his first fully realized board game. Momentum represents not just a game but the fulfillment of a long-held dream—bringing his creative vision to life and sharing it with others who love strategy and innovation as much as he does.
