the Developer

Anssi Janttonen is a student at Haaga-Helia university of applied sciences, Finland. He developed a taste for board games approximately 5 years ago when his fiance introduced him to the local board game scene of Joensuu. He has since committed much of his time to the hobby and has long dreamt of making a game of his own. Now that dream is realized in Momentum.
